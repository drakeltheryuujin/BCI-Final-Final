WHAT IS BLUE CARBON?

  • What is blue carbon
  • What are the ecosystems
  • Seasgrasses
  • Tidal Marshes

"Blue carbon" describes the carbon found in coastal and marine ecosystems. The capacity of the global oceans to store carbon is a topic of ongoing research. The Blue Carbon Initiative is currently focused on three coastal ecosystems -mangroves, tidal marshes and seagrasses- that are increasingly understood for their ability to accumulate, store and sequester carbon.
